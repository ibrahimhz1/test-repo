About ICMBNT 2025

Scope of the Conference

  • The International Conference on Multidisciplinary Breakthroughs and NextGen Technologies (ICMBNT 2025) is designed to integrate perspectives from Science, Technology, Medical and Healthcare, Management, Social Sciences, Education, Sports, and Environmental Studies to develop holistic solutions for global issues.


  • In a rapidly evolving digital-first business world, global organizations are highly influenced by next-generation technologies. Future technological advancements, developments, and innovations enabled by the internet, software, and services are known as next-generation technologies. These include advanced robotics, AI, IoT, RPA, quantum computing, 3-D printing, 5G wireless networks, virtual reality and augmented reality, and blockchain.


  • Next-generation technologies are paving the way for network-enabled, miniature, and fully automated machines. Although enterprise applications based on such technologies are still in the nascent stages of development, they are gradually beginning to drive innovation strategies in business, and the overall impact of these technologies is expected to multiply over the coming years.


  • ICMBNT 2025 will be a central hub for esteemed research experts worldwide, offering unparalleled opportunities to network, gain invaluable insights, showcase their potential, present significant research findings, and receive due credit and recognition for their diligent work. Join us in this collaborative endeavor, where the intersections of knowledge spark new dimensions in research and propel us collectively toward innovative solutions and breakthroughs.

Why Attend?

  • Engage with top researchers and industry experts.
  • Showcase your research and receive recognition.
  • Gain insights into future technological trends.
  • Network with professionals from diverse fields.

Conference Highlights

  • Keynote speeches from distinguished speakers.
  • Interactive panel discussions.
  • Technical paper presentations.
  • Workshops on emerging technologies.
