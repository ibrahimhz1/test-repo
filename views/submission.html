Paper Submission

We invite scholars, researchers, and professionals to submit their insightful papers and abstracts. Join us in building a platform that celebrates diverse perspectives and fosters collaboration across a spectrum of research domains.


Submission Guidelines

  • The manuscript must be within 12 pages and in double-column format.
  • Include the paper title, author(s) details, affiliations, email, and keywords on the first page.
  • All submissions will be screened for plagiarism. Any identified plagiarism will result in rejection.
  • Accepted manuscripts must follow reviewer suggestions before final submission.
  • Final camera-ready papers must be submitted before the deadline.

Important Dates

  • Manuscript Submission Deadline: 15 March 2025
  • Acceptance Notification: 25 March 2025
  • Final Registration Date: 5 April 2025
  • Conference Dates: 26-27 April 2025

Submit Your Paper

Authors must submit their manuscripts via email to: icmbnt2025@gmail.com
